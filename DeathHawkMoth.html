What are they about?

These are the death hawk moths they are known for thier skull that is on the top of the thorax. They are also well known in the tattooing community because of the symbolism of them. The symbolism of them is that they are an omen of death and mystery. The scientific of the Death Hawk Moth is Acherontia Atropos. This name is apart of the greek mythology of the underworld.

Cultural/History Significants

The history and the cultural significants of this Hawk moth is very dark but it goes back to greek mythology and to King George III.
